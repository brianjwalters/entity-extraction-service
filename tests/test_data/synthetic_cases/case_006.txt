SUPERIOR COURT OF CALIFORNIA, COUNTY OF SAN DIEGO
Case No. 37-2023-00123456-CU-BC-CTL

JOHNSON v. SMITH CONSTRUCTION, INC.

Plaintiff Sarah Johnson filed this breach of contract action against Defendant Smith Construction, Inc. on February 1, 2023. The complaint alleges that Defendant failed to complete residential construction work as required under a written contract dated June 15, 2022. Plaintiff seeks damages of $125,000. Trial is set for November 15, 2024, before Judge Robert Williams.

Attorney Michael Chen represents Plaintiff. Attorney Lisa Martinez represents Defendant.
